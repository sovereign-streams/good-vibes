What is Good Vibes?

Algorithmic Sovereignty for Everyone

Today's platforms optimize your feed for engagement — time on site, emotional activation, ad yield. You have zero control over the algorithm. Good Vibes inverts this.

You Define the Algorithm

Set your own content weights, emotional filters, session boundaries, and rhythm design. Your algorithm serves your goals, not an advertiser's.

Sessions, Not Feeds

Good Vibes doesn't serve a feed. It composes a session — a designed sequence with emotional pacing, like a playlist with narrative structure.

Open Protocol

Built on the Stream Exchange Protocol (SEP) — an open standard for exchanging metadata-driven content between sovereign providers and consumers.

Privacy by Design

State lives on the consumer side. Providers see only what you choose to share. No behavioral profiling. No fingerprinting. You choose your disclosure level.

How It Works

From Raw Content to Designed Sessions

Four stages transform platform content into personally curated cognitive experiences.

Enrich

Raw content metadata is processed by an LLM-powered pipeline, producing semantically tagged records with categories, emotional tone, and scoring dimensions.

Curate

Providers apply their published guardrails and serve enriched metadata via SEP. Good Vibes filters for cognitive flourishing — no rage-bait, no humiliation content.

Compose

Your Personal Algorithm Engine applies your weights, filters, and session rules. It composes a session arc — a designed sequence with emotional pacing and rhythm.

Experience

The consumer shell renders your session. Content is lazy-loaded from origin sources. When the session ends, it ends — no infinite scroll.

The Problem

The Attention Extraction Economy

Current platforms optimize for engagement — not for your wellbeing. The algorithm is designed to keep you scrolling, not to make you better.

Involuntary Neurological Shaping

Feed ordering influences mood, identity, attention patterns, political views, and self-perception. Algorithmic feeds correlate with increased anxiety, depression, and reduced attention span.

Zero User Control

You can't tell TikTok "30% fitness, 20% humor, zero rage-bait, cap at 15 minutes." There is no system that lets you design your own feed. Until now.

Existing Solutions Fall Short

Screen time limits? You switch apps. Content blockers? Blunt instruments. "Take a break" reminders? Easily dismissed. Algorithm transparency laws? Knowing it exists doesn't give you control.

The Core Question

How much of your thinking is yours, and how much is shaped by an algorithm you didn't choose? Good Vibes gives you the answer — and the alternative.

Internet 4.0: Seamless Sovereignty

Web 1.0 was read-only. Web 2.0 gave us platforms. Web 3.0 decentralized the ledger. Internet 4.0 decentralizes influence — giving individuals control over the algorithms that shape their cognitive environment.

Core Concepts

The Building Blocks

Good Vibes introduces a protocol layer and a set of components that work together to enable algorithmic sovereignty.

Stream Exchange Protocol

An open protocol defining how Stream Providers and Stream Consumers exchange metadata-driven content through structured negotiation. Content-agnostic, privacy-preserving, lightweight.

Enrichment Layer

An LLM-powered pipeline that transforms raw content metadata into semantically tagged records — categories, emotional tone, energy level, skill transfer scores, and session fit indicators.

Personal Algorithm Engine
